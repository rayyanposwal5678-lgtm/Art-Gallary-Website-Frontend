Our Story

About Lumière

Founded in 1897, Lumière has been a beacon of artistic excellence for over a century. Nestled in the heart of Paris, our gallery has witnessed the rise of Impressionism, the bold strokes of Expressionism, and the digital revolution of contemporary art. We believe that art is not merely seen — it is felt, experienced, and lived. Every piece in our collection has been chosen not for its market value, but for its ability to move the human spirit.

Today, Lumière continues its mission to bridge the timeless and the contemporary, hosting over 45 exhibitions annually and nurturing emerging talent alongside established masters. Our doors remain open to all who seek beauty, meaning, and connection through the universal language of art.
